" Programming today is a race between software engineers striving to build bigger and better idiot-proof programs, and the Universe trying to produce bigger and better idiots. So far, the Universe is winning."

A quote from Rich Cook

One of the best practices in software engineering is to build codes that is easy to read and understand, and create an application that even a person that has little knowledge can operate it.

As people became dependent on technology, the expectations for more user-friendly applications are high.
